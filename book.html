Whisper of Elenvar

In the forgotten kingdom of Elenvar—where rivers run backward and the stars no longer speak—a cloaked wanderer arrives bearing a sword that remembers. The kingdom, once ruled by song and shadow, has been cursed into silence, its name erased from the tongues of men. The wanderer, known only as Seren Vale, carries a blade forged beneath the ruins of Virehold. It drinks moonlight and memory, and it cannot be drawn without cost. Seren seeks the last surviving Archivist, Maireth of the Hollow, whose forbidden texts may hold the key to restoring Elenvar—or burying it forever. As Seren unravels the kingdom’s lost history, they must navigate fractured alliances, spectral guardians, and the haunting truth behind their own exile. But in Elenvar, even memory is treacherous—and some whispers were never meant to be heard.
